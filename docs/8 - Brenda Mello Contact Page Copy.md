### How Booking Works

The process is simple and stress-free:

1. **Reach out** — Fill in the inquiry form or send me a message with the details of what you need.
2. **We connect** — I'll reply to talk through your vision, answer questions, and check availability.
3. **We plan your look** — Together we'll confirm the service, date, and any details that make your experience special.
4. **You're booked** — Once everything's set, I'll send a confirmation so you know exactly what to expect.

No pressure, no rush — just a warm, easy way to get started.

---

### What to Include When You Reach Out

The more I know, the better I can help. When you message me, try to share:

- **The service you're interested in** — wedding, event, or graduation makeup and hair
- **Your date** (or rough timing) — especially for weddings and events
- **Location** — where the service or styling will take place
- **A little about your vision** — the style, mood, or result you're hoping for
- **Anything else that matters to you** — questions, concerns, or special requests

Don't worry if you're not sure about every detail. Even a quick note is enough to get the ball rolling, and we'll fill in the rest together.
